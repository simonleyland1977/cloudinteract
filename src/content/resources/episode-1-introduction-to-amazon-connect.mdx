Welcome to the first episode of the CloudInteract Connect Podcast! In this episode we introduce ourselves and cover the basics of Amazon Connect: its capabilities and features. We discuss who Amazon Connect is for, how to get it and pay for it, and the pitfalls of "just turning it on". Alex has a great example of a customer that saw significant savings using the auto-scale capabilities that Connect can bring to the contact centre.
