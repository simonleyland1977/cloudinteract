In this episode of ACP, hosts Tom and Alex are joined by special guest Ajmal Mahmood, Customer Experience Director at [Omningage](https://www.omningage.cloud/). Ajmal shares his extensive background in the contact centre industry, discusses the building of Omningage's Amazon Connect-based product [Rocket CCaaS](https://rocketccaas.cloud/), and highlights the innovative features and benefits of their solutions. The conversation also delves into the impact of AI and the evolving needs of customer and agent experiences in the contact centre world. Don't miss this insightful discussion on leveraging Amazon Connect for superior customer engagement!
