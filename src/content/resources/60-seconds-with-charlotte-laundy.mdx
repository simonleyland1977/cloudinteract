## **Meet Charlotte Laundy**

**![Charlotte](https://145219679.fs1.hubspotusercontent-eu1.net/hubfs/145219679/People%20Pictures/Charlotte.jpg)Meet Charlotte Laundy, CloudInteract’s Platform Engineer -** master of Amazon Connect, problem-solver extraordinaire, and proud dog walker. Whether she’s debugging code or dreaming of pasta (her one true love), Charlotte is all about making the complex simple for customers. With a love for unique zebra stripes and a wish to speak every language, she brings a blend of creativity, curiosity, and collaboration to the team - always with a smile and maybe a nod to the IT Crowd.

#### Favourite Quote: 

“Have you tried turning it off and back on again.” IT Crowd

#### How long have you worked for CloudInteract? 

##### 1 Year 

#### What was your first day like? 

##### Like any first day, I felt nervous, but what really stood out was how welcoming everyone was. I quickly felt like part of the team, which made settling in much easier.   
 

#### What does working here mean to you? 

Working here means I have the opportunity to collaborate with a team of highly skilled individuals in a positive and friendly environment. That kind of teamwork is really important to me. 

#### How would you describe what you do? 

##### As a Contact Centre Platform Engineer primarily working with Amazon Connect, I focus on configuring the platform to meet a customer's business needs and integrating it with other AWS services and applications. I troubleshoot any technical issues that arise and at times assist with running training sessions for new users of the platform.  

#### What's the best part of your job? 

##### Problem-solving!  

#### Describe a typical day... 

##### A typical day for me starts with walking my dogs in the morning, After that, I jump into work with stand-up meetings to discuss our priorities. Throughout the day, I take care of troubleshooting calls and focus on development tasks.  

### The Fun Part 

#### If you could have one superpower, what would it be? 

##### The ability to understand and speak any language. 

#### What's your favourite food? 

##### A tricky one… anything pasta related! 

#### If you could be an animal, what would you be and why? 

##### I’d probably choose to be a zebra because I love that each one has unique stripes. I think that’s pretty cool!
